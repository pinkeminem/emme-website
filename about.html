about

musician

emme is an experimental electronic artist working at the intersection of noise, ambient, and industrial music. performances blur the line between sonic exploration and visceral experience, creating immersive soundscapes that challenge conventional boundaries.

notable appearances include CTM Festival (Berlin), LUFF (Lausanne), Venice Biennale, Fekete Zaj, and Creepy Teepee. the work draws from a deep engagement with texture, silence, and the physicality of sound.

stage technician & lighting designer

beyond music, emme brings technical expertise to theatrical and live event production. trained in lighting design and stage management, with experience spanning experimental theatre, live performance, and large-scale events.

  • lighting design & programming
  • stage management
  • production coordination
  • technical direction
  • live event production
  • dmx & lighting control systems
